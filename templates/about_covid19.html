COVID-19

The outbreak of COVID-19 epidemic is threating human existence everyday with new challenges since December 2019. The virus causes fever, cough, fatigue and mild to severe respiratory complications that has killed more than 60 thousand people around the world till 5th April 2020. It has infected more than 1.2 million people around the world till today. According WHO health official 95 percent of patient are in Mild Condition and 5% are in Critical condition. It will infect much more according to World Health Organization (WHO) and researchers around the globe. The World Health organization declared the outbreak as a pandemic on march 11 as the virus reached to every corner of the globe. Furthermore, recent reports expose an astonishing case fatality rate of 61.5% for critical cases, increasing sharply with age and for patients with underlying previous health cases. Both the reach and severity of cases is putting great pressure on the medical services, and readily lead to a shortage of intensive care resources.
